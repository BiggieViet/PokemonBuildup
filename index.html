Pokémon Buildup Showdown - Official Rules

Welcome to Pokémon Buildup Showdown!

This format begins with each player selecting one Pokémon from a set of three randomly generated options. Each Pokémon is assigned four random moves. Players then progress through battles to earn points and expand or enhance their team.

Team Construction & Progression

• At the start of play, each player must select one of the three Pokémon presented. A mulligan may only be used if all players mutually agree to reroll the set.

• All Pokémon used in battle must be nicknamed.

• After each completed battle, players add one additional randomly generated Pokémon from another list of three to their team and may spend earned points to:
  - Learn new moves
  - Change abilities
  - Assign held items
  - Allocate EVs
  - Apply cosmetic upgrades such as shininess

Point costs are listed in the Shop section.

Scoring System

• 1 point is awarded for each of your Pokémon that were knocked out.
• 3 points are awarded for each of your Pokémon that remains alive at the end of the match.

Match Format

• Teams of 1-3 Pokémon compete under National Dex Ubers 1v1 rules.
• Teams of 4 or more Pokémon compete under National Dex Doubles 2v2 rules.

Season Clauses

• Species Clause - The same Pokémon species will not be generated more than once for a player. Alternate forms (e.g., regional variants) are permitted.

• Pseudo‑Legendary Clause (600+ BST) - Pokémon with a Base Stat Total of 600 or higher are specially marked when rolled. If a player selects a pseudo‑legendary, no additional pseudo‑legendaries will appear for that player for the remainder of the run.

• Illegal Move Clause - If Pokémon Showdown determines that a generated move is illegal for a Pokémon, that move is replaced with an Any‑Move Token, allowing the player to select any legal move in its place.
